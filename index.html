Events

Annual Fundraiser

Raising money for school across South America has been a huge tradition for this company which involves walking around the community from door to door asking for money for supporting the low income families in South America. We also run bake sales across the Ontario School Board of Education in order to raise awareness of this crisis.

Company

The company which has been around for over 10 years, specializes in creating a vast amount of different chocolate flavours that combine traditional chocolate as well as creamy milk texture. The Dark Chocolate comes straight from the sugar cane forest of west Africa as well as from the Sahara desert. Our chocolates are extra rich and they have a melt in your mouth caramel peanut crunch flavor that will energize you for the rest of the day.
